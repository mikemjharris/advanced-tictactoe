Ultimate TicTacToe

Like normal TicTacToe but bigger and better.

  • The aim is to win on the big board.
  • To make a square yours you need to win the small game within that square.
  • The last played square on a small board determines which big square is played next.
  • e.g. Say O's plays the middle big square. On the small board within they play middle left, X has to make a move in the small game inside the big board's middle left square
